1. Background && Research Problems

  • hallucination types: object existence hallucination, object attribute hallucination, object relationship hallucination
  • POPE work may suffer an incomplete assessment of detailed captions.
    • so introduce CCEval
  • what are the underlying causes of object hallucination ?
  • how to control the extent of expressed hallucination or parametric knowledge?
    • propose HallE-Switch
    • such guessing is necessary depends on diff downstream tasks, so it’s better to control hallucination rather than to eliminate.

captioning can be seen as a form of information mapping or translation:

  • objects successfully grounded by vision encoder form accurate associations → contextual knowledge
  • objects in language that vision encoder failed to ground create word-word semantic associations. →parametric knowledge

2.3 control hallucination: HallE-Switch to control extent of parametric knowledge
