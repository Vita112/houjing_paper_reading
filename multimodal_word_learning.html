LM-Switch: Lightweight Language Model Conditioning in Word Embedding Space

Posted on Tue, Nov 7, 2023

Takeaway

可控性文本生成指的是 根据不同的控制目标来生成文本；这篇文章不是训练一个具体的任务特定的model，而是提供了一个plug-and-play的条件控制，因而训练好的LM-Switch可以被迁移至其他不同size的LMs。

What I’m thinking

1. Background && Research Problems

it’s desirable to steer a language model’s generation according to different conditions. scenarios like to mitigate bias, manage risks and eusuring fair and unbiased representation.

controlling LM generations have attracted increasing attention, and attempts are made like

  • applying attribute classifiers or heuristic constrains at decoding time
  • see generation as an optimization over word embeddings
  • prompt-based control
  • discover steering vectors or tokens : most related

LM Detoxification aims to address the systematic biases embeded in language models. existing methods contain:

  • automatically obtain cleaner data
  • modify model workflow to explcitly accommodate the bias factors
  • manipulating embedding space
  • via LM controlling: most related
    • Dexperts: Decoding-time controlled text generation with experts and anti-experts : used in experiments
